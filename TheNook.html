The Nook

Review

The Nook is a restaurant located in the heart of the funk zone. Sporting a hefty 4.4 out of five stars, this great eatery is a necessary experience for anyone who is visiting Santa Barbara, or even just looking for a place to eat dinner. This establishment has a vast menu filled with California cuisine that will make your mouth water. Not only this, but the nook is working in conjunction with 2 other local bars to provide the finest drink and one of the most fun environments in Santa barbara. And for those nature lovers out there, Santa barbara’s stunning beach is only a couple blocks away. The Nook truly is the place to be in Santa Barbara for both expertly crafted food and a wonderfully exhilarating environment.
