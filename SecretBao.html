Secret Bao

Best Items

Japchae, Bibimbap, Hamachi Poke, and the KFC Bao.

Review

Secret Bao is an excelent Asian fusion restaurant just one block from State Street. It has an ever changning menu, allowing you to get something new every time you come. They always have baos which are very good, but I feel the highlight of the menu is the mains. Every single main on the menu is delicious. They range from strictly Asian dishes to Asian fusion and many dishes of their own creation. My best meals here have been sharing a bunch of mains with my friends. Secret Bao supplies a complex range of flavors, both modern, traditional, and fusion dishes, and has great service and presentation. They truly put love and much thought into their food and deserve to be called one of the best restaurants in Santa Barbara.
