Craft Ramen

Review

Craft Ramen is one of many more recently established Japanese restraunts in town. Although the restraunt is so popular, its not hard to get a table. Usually you walk in and they seat you immediately. Everything on the menu is good to great, and all while at an affordable cost. My favorite is the Pork Chashu bowl, which is the best bang for your buck when looking at the food to price ratio. This restraunt has been open for a few years and I would be surprised to see it gone soon, so no rush to try to get there to grab a bit, but once you do you'll be hooked.

Review by:
Paulo Lange
